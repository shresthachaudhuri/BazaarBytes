Returns & Refunds Policy

Eligibility for Returns

Items can be returned within 30 days of purchase if they are unused, in original packaging, and in the same condition as received. Please ensure all tags and accessories are included.

Refund Process

Once your return is received and inspected, we will notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund. If approved, the refund will be processed within 7-10 business days to the original payment method.

Non-Returnable Items

Custom orders, perishable goods, and digital products are non-returnable unless defective. Please contact us if you receive a damaged item.

Contact Us

For any questions, please reach out to our support team via Live Chat Support or email us at support@bazaarbytes.com.
